Keybinds

Camera Movement¶

  • W A S D: Forward Left Backward Right
  • C: Downwards
  • Spacebar: Upwards
  • RMB : Activate mouse look
    • While this button is held, moving your mouse around will move the camera
  • Shift: Speed up camera

Brushes/Cursor Tools¶

  • 1 - 7: hotbar slots
  • LMB : Activate tool
  • E + SW : increase/decrease tool scale
  • Q + SW : increase/decrease tool strength
